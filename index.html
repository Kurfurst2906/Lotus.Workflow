Lotus.Workflow

Lotus.Workflow is an internal workflow automation application built with Google Apps Script. The application is designed to support internal operational processes by managing, processing, and visualizing spreadsheet-based workflow data.

Access to this application is restricted to authorized users only. It is not intended for public or consumer use.
